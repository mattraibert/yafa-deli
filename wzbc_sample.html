HELP

The "listen" links are so-called 'M3U playlist' files (small text files containing info about the audio) that programs like iTunes, QuickTime Player, WinAmp and Window Media Player can open. If your computer doesn't play the archive automatically when you click the link you may be able to download the file (if it didn't already) and open it in one of those player programs.

Happily, it's normally possible to configure your computer so that the links play automatically. But, sadly, I don't have any advice on how to do it.
